FAQs

What types of exercises are best for beginners?

Beginners should start with low-impact exercises like walking, cycling, or bodyweight exercises to build endurance and strength gradually.

How can I improve my mental health through physical activity?

Regular exercise releases endorphins, which can reduce stress, anxiety, and depression while improving mood and self-esteem.

What are some healthy meal options for weight loss?

Focus on lean proteins, whole grains, vegetables, and healthy fats. Examples include grilled chicken with quinoa, salads with nuts, and veggie stir-fries.
